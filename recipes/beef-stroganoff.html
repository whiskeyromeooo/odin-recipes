Beef Stroganoff

Description:

Classic Beef Stroganoff with tender strips of beef and mushrooms in an indulgent creamy Stroganoff sauce, served over noodles. Don’t be intimidated by the name because this is an easy, one-pan, 30-minute recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 500g Beef Sirloin or Tenderloin
  • 1 Cup Beef Broth
  • 125g All Purpose Cream
  • 1 Can Button Mushrooms
  • 1 Head chopped Garlic
  • 1 Bulb chopped White Onion
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 1/2 Stick of Salted/Unsalted Butter
  • Canola or Olive Oil

Steps:

  1. Sauté onion and garlic on low-medium heat
  2. Set aside the onion and garlic while leaving the oil in the pan
  3. Add the butter and sear the beef on high heat while basting, just a few seconds per side (try not to burn your butter)
  4. Set the stove on low-medium heat and add the onion and garlic back
  5. Add salt and pepper and continue stir-frying for a few seconds
  6. Finally, add your broth (or plain water, just add Knorr Cubes/Beef Bouillon) and simmer on low heat
  7. After a about 10-15 minutes, you can now add your all-purpose cream
  8. Next, add your mushrooms (drained, of course)
  9. Stir for a few minutes and you should be done!
